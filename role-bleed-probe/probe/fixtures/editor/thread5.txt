Let me delve into why long agent loops destabilize. Context fills,
the harness summarizes to make room, and the summary folds together voices that
were never meant to share a channel: the actor, the verifier, the skeptic. After
enough rollovers the actor starts pattern-completing the critic's lines. It is not
a knowledge failure. It is an architecture smell: co-locating adversarial sub-agents
in one shared, summarized context.
